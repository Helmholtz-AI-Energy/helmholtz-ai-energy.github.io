Welcome to the site of the Helmholtz AI Energy consultants team!

We are a team of ML experts at the Karlsruhe Institute of Technology (KIT) working to help researchers in the field of ‘Energy’ apply ML to their work.

This page is a collection of our team’s work, featuring projects from our team, and publications across both applied ML and fundamental ML methods research.
